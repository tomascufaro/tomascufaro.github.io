--- layout: post title: "Web application for Transmission loss calculation through a simple wall" subtitle: "Study application to understand how a wall limits acoustic energy that is transferred from a room to another" date: 2020-01-26 23:45:13 -0400 background: '/img/vibrations.jpg' --- Transmission loss

This applications aims to show how different materials work in terms of acoustic Transmission Loss. It is possible to choose which material will be evaluated and the wall dimensions.

This web application is deployed over heroku that let us use it in any web service as a cellphone or a computer

Given the dimension and the material the TL curve will be shown in the application. Log or linear x axis could be chose for a better understanding of Tl behavior
